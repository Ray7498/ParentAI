School Transfer Procedure in Germany

If a family moves to another city, children can transfer schools.

Steps usually include:

1. Contact the new school in the destination city.
2. Provide the most recent report cards (Zeugnisse).
3. Submit proof of residence (Anmeldung).
4. The school administration reviews available places.

Transfers between cities such as Heilbronn and Munich usually take between one and three weeks depending on school capacity.
