Parent Guide for Families in Heilbronn

Several secondary schools are located in Heilbronn including:

Robert-Mayer-Gymnasium
Focus on science and STEM education.

Justinus-Kerner-Gymnasium
Known for language programs and cultural activities.

Elly-Heuss-Knapp-Gymnasium
Offers strong arts and music education.

Dammrealschule
Provides practical education and technical subjects.

Parents can contact schools through the school office or online portals.

Parent teacher conferences (Elternsprechtag) are usually held twice per year.
