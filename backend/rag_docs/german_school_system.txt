German School System Overview

After primary school (Grundschule), students in Germany usually move into one of several types of schools.

Gymnasium
Gymnasium prepares students for university education and ends with the Abitur qualification.

Realschule
Realschule provides a mix of academic and practical education and usually ends after grade 10.

Hauptschule
Hauptschule focuses more on vocational preparation and practical skills.

Gesamtschule
Comprehensive schools combine different academic tracks.

German Grading System

1 – Very Good
2 – Good
3 – Satisfactory
4 – Pass
5 – Poor
6 – Fail

Grades 1–4 are passing grades.
